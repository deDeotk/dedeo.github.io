Re: “Tour of Masajid,” The Muslim Observer (3 July 2013)

I wonder what a similar tour of churches around my city (and to bring it home, a tour of NAPARC-variety churches—yea, a tour of my church) would reveal about the degree of care for the local community exhibited by Christians.

Perhaps some meditation upon Matthew 5:16 is in order. To use Calvin’s commentary as a prod for reflection: do I regulate my life (including my social ethic) so as “to excite all to glorify God.”
