One does not understand the gospel of revelation and redemption through Jesus Christ if it does not answer for him the question: “What shall I do?” A theology that does not call for and strengthen ethical activity is certainly not a Christian theology. The ethical question is the supreme question of all life.

—Emil Brunner, “The Problem of Ethics,” in The Theology of Crisis (New York: Scribner’s, 1929), 68.
